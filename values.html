Our Core Values
  • Quality: We ensure clean and fresh milk in every delivery.
  • Integrity: We act honestly and transparently in all our operations.
  • Innovation: We seek better and more efficient ways to collect, process, and deliver dairy products.
  • Teamwork: We collaborate closely with farmers and partners.
  • Community Impact: We help farmers grow and create rural job opportunities.
  • Sustainability: We promote environmentally friendly and long-term dairy development.

Quality

As our value is to deliver quality milk, it is our target to make sure our milk passes the following tests.

delivery must done well : we avoid delays so that we make sure that our milk get on the milk collection center on the time to take the quality test early

Alcohol Test: Checks milk stability and freshness, ensuring it's free from acidity and suitable for processing.

Resazurin Test: This test helps us estimate the microbial load in the milk. By observing color changes, we can quickly assess its hygienic quality.

Temperature Control: Proper temperature is crucial. We check every batch to ensure it's correctly chilled to preserve freshness and prevent bacterial growth.

Water Adulteration Test: To guarantee purity, we test for added water. This ensures you receive 100% pure milk with all its natural nutrients.
