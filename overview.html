Overview

Mediate Dairy Group Industry will be a modern milk processing facility in Rwanda designed to:

  • Collect fresh milk from local farmers (Shangasha and Rushaki sectors)
  • Process milk into yogurt, butter, and other dairy products
  • Reduce milk wastage
  • Connect farmers directly to the market
